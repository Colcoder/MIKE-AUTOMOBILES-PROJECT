ABOUT

MIKE AUTOMOBILES GARAGE has been around for over ten years now,Michael Otieno and Matthews Ogutu being the founders of the garage. It started as a small garage in DRO-KADERO area in Eldoret town and over the years it has grown to serve customers from as far as KITALE,NANDI,MARAKWET and many other environs of Eldoret.
